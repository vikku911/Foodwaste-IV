Food Waste Dataset



Introduction

In recent years, the issue of food waste has gained significant attention as the world grapples with the challenge of providing sustenance to a growing population. The wastage of food not only squanders valuable resources but also exacerbates environmental problems such as greenhouse gas emissions and landfill pollution. In response to this pressing concern, our project aims to develop a comprehensive Food Waste Management System (FWMS) that will tackle the issue of food waste at its core. The Food Waste Management System project seeks to establish an innovative and sustainable solution to address the various stages of food waste generation, management, and reduction. By implementing an integrated system that encompasses efficient food production, optimized distribution, responsible consumption, and waste diversion techniques, our objective is to minimize the amount of food waste generated while maximizing its potential for reuse, recycling, and recovery.
